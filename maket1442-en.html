Ecosystem in your body:
Microbiome

Our bodies are home to trillions of microorganisms that accompany us from the moment we are born, which are of indisputable importance to our health and quality of life. These microscopic creatures, their genetic materials, genome products and their interactions with the environment make up our microbiome as a whole.

The microbiome world that exists within us contacts us with the metabolites they produce, although we are not aware of them. If a microbiome is diverse and balanced, it is the biggest factor in establishing this communication in favor of our health.

A balanced microbiome protects us against metabolic problems, autoimmune diseases, diseases associated with the digestive system and mental problems, and increases our existing quality of life by contributing to physiological and psychological recovery.

After the microbiome is profiled, ENBIOSIS's artificial intelligence technology combines the microbiome profile with the data provided by the scientific literature on microbiome-nutrition and prepares a personalized nutrition guide.
