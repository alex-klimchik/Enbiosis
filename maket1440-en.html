What is the microbiome?

Our bodies are home to trillions of microorganisms that accompany us from the moment we are born, which are of indisputable importance to our health and quality of life. Together with these microscopic creatures, their genetic materials, their interactions with the environment and the whole of the habitat they live in make up our microbiome. The vast majority of our microbiome is composed of bacteria. For this reason, the distribution and diversity of our bacteria is critical in the balance of the microbiome

How does the process work?

  • Order enbiosis microbiome kit

    order from www.enbiosis.com, sign up from the website with the code in your kit and complete the survey questions.

  • Sampling

    Follow the steps in the kit delivered by cargo to your address and easily take the sample required for your microbiome analysis. Then send it to the address in the box for free.

  • Laborotory analysis

    Your sample is processed in our metagenome laboratory and sequenced with 16S rRNA next generation sequencing method. The resulting data is analyzed and reported in detail with our artificial intelligence algorithms.

  • Enbiosis Personalized Nutrition Guide

    Your reports will be checked by our experts and then delivered to you. Your nutritional scores are interpreted by ENBIOISIS dietitians and presented with a free 30-minute dietitian interview.
